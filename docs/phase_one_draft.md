# Phase 1: Embodying the Problem Domain Publishing Lifecycle

## Primary Objectives

* **Service-Oriented:** Reconciling the technical requirements of Responsible AI (RAI) and Explainable AI (XAI) with the fundamental objectives of service to all human populations (including the undocumented and unrepresented) and the broader planetary biosphere.
* **Global Alignment:** Establishing a framework where the preservation of "one human race" and "one planet Earth" (as defined by the HLOS Duty of Care and Ecocide provisions) serve as the primary sensitivities for success in any machine learning deployment.
* **Lifecycle Resolutions:** Inviting contributors to help define and refine the protocols for publishing these specialized implementations. This includes establishing how the Open Source Imperative—integrating ethics, responsibility, and explainability natively—can be formulaically applied across diverse technical ecosystems and cloud infrastructures.

---

## Scope & Constraints

* **Open Participation:** This phase is a call for "anyone and everyone" to participate in resolving the publishing lifecycle for these prime objectives.
* **Focused Discourse:** Discussion is strictly limited to the technical and organizational methodologies required to foster global trust, alignment, and sound environmentalism through AI.
* **Architectural Boundaries:** This documentation serves as the public-facing collaborative engine while strictly maintaining the necessary physical architectural workspace for sovereign, localized development and transparent cloud deployment.
